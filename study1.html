Welcome to the PENTOMINO pilot study!

We invite participants to an online study from KTH and the University of Potsdam.

In this experiment, you will assess natural language instructions to pick pentomino objects.

It is important that you can clearly hear the spoken instructions. You may eiter use headphones or your computer speakers in a silent environment. You can only do the study on a computer, but not on a mobile device (tablet, phone, etc.).

It is also important that you try to do as few mistakes as possible in the task. If any problems appear with the interface, please let us know before you complete the task.

The study will last 10-15 minutes and can be done at your home or office at your time of preference.


If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact Dimosthenis Kontogiorgos at diko@kth.se or Jana Götze at jana.goetze@uni-potsdam.de.

Informed consent form

In this study conducted by KTH and University of Potsdam you will assess the suitability of instructions to Pentomino objects. It is important that you are fluent in English. The study is expected to last 10-15 minutes and is entirely voluntary.

We collect your mouse movements and click actions as well as your responses to the questions provided. The data will only be used for research purposes and may be shared with other researchers. If you have any questions you can ask Dimosthenis Kontogiorgos at diko@kth.se or Jana Götze at jana.goetze@uni-potsdam.de.
